About Me

I am now a Tenure-track Professor at School of Data Science, Fudan University. I was a Postdoctoral Researcher and Research Assistant Professor in The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, working with Prof. Tong Zhang. Before that I was also a Research Scientist in Tencent AI Lab. I received my Ph.D. on Computer Science at Zhejiang University under my supervisors Prof. Xiaofei He(何晓飞教授) and Prof. Deng Cai（蔡登教授）. I was a Joint PhD student in The University of Michigan, Ann Arbor under the supervision of Prof. Jieping Ye and Prof. Jie Wang. I have broad interests in machine learning, deep learning algorithms, federated learning etc. My current research focuses on developing algorithms to improve the training/inference efficiency and generalization ability of deep neural networks and their applications in other areas (e.g., computer vision). I have published more than 40 papers in peer-reviewed top-tier journal papers (JMLR, IEEE TPAMI, IEEE TKDE, etc.) and conference papers (ICML, NeurIPS, CVPR, etc.).

Our team with Prof. Xiaofei He and Cheng Jin recruits several self-motivated students (Ph.Ds., masters, undergraduate students, interns, gap year students) to work on model compression, federated learning, efficient training/finetuning/inference algorithms for LLM and diffusion models etc. If you are interested in my group, please contact me as soon as possible.
